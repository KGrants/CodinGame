You and your friends decide to go to the fun fair. As a fan of great sensation, you decide to go to "the great circle", an attraction which rotate on itself.
Given the length of the arm in meter and the number of revolutions per minute, you must output the speed at which you are moving in meter/second rounded to 2 decimal places.

How to get the linear speed?:
your speed is equal to the circumference of the circle you travel multiplied by the revolutions per second

Input
Line 1 : The length l of the arm (in meters)
Line 2 : The rpm r of the attraction

Output
Line 1 : Your linear speed in m/s

Input
2
8

Output
1.68
