This is a "Shortest" mode of code of clash.

You are given a int n of how many numbers there are in the sequence and sequence of numbers seperated by space. 
You have to output the sum of minimum and maximum numbers of the sequence.

Example:
Input:
2
1 1

Output:
2

Input:
3
1 8 6

Output:
9

Input:
6
32 19 5 95 46 24

Output:
100
