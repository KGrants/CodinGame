Barber Bob always nods politely while his clients explain what haircut they want, then proceeds to cut 1 cm off each hair until it is at least 40% shorter than its starting length.

Given the lengths of all the hairs on a client's head, determine how many strands of hair are on the floor when Bob finishes.

Input
Line 1: The Number of different hair lengths
Next N lines: The number of hairs of a given length, space separated

Output
The number of hairs on the floor.

Constraints
2 ≤ N,l ≤ 100
1 ≤ h ≤ 100,000

Example
Input
3
25000 6.5
50000 12
25000 10.5

Output
450000
