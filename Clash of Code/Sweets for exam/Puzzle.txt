You are given a number n of students who have passed the exam. 
Teacher will give out candies to each student that has passed the exam. 
Teached will distribute the candies the following way: 1 candy for first student that passed, 3 to the second, 5 to the fifth and so on. 

How many packs of candies teacher has to buy so that he would have enough candies for all students who passed exam. 

Example:
Input:
4

Output:
2
